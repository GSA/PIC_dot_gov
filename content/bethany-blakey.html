Bethany Blakey

Director of Programs & Initiatives
Bethany.Blakey@pic.gov
  • 

Leaders Delivery Network, Modern Government Management Traits Initiative, Fed-preneur Initiative, Performance Enthusiast Program, Performance Onboarding Initiative, Performance Ambassador Program, Strategic Narrative & Effective Presentations Initiative, Strategies & Tactics Coaching, Developmental Assignments to the PIC.

Bethany Blakey has 20 years of experience in state, local, and federal government. She served as neighborhood policy coordinator for the Mayor of the City of Pittsburgh, sole staff overseeing operations and capital projects for the Stadium Authority of the City of Pittsburgh, a Presidential Management Fellow for multiple agencies in the Department of Justice, co-lead for the Vice President’s Crime Mapping and Data Driven Management Task Force, and Deputy Program Manager on the design, launch and implementation of IdeaHub, a federated ideation platform which earned recognition from Harvard’s Ash Center as a top Government Innovation. Bethany earned a Master of Public Administration in Public Management and Policy, a Master of Social Work in Community Organization and Planning, and Master’s Certificate in Nonprofit Management from the University of Pittsburgh.
