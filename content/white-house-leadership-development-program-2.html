Nomination and Selection Process

This program is open only to career GS-15 and equivalent civil service employees. Agencies can now choose the application process:

  1. Agencies conduct an internal vetting process and forward top six candidates.
  2. Self-nomination with supervisor recommendation and supervisor's approval.

Agency Nominations

Interested agencies may nominate six candidates. Agencies determine their own internal processes to select nominees and submit them to the WHLD Program for further consideration.

SELF-NOMINATIONS

Self-nomination with supervisor recommendation and supervisor's approval. WHLDP program will be responsible for vetting these candidates. WHLDP provides vetting to the self-nominated candidates, putting forward a maximum of 6 candidates per agency for interviews.

Selection Process

After reviewing formal nominations from agencies, the WHLD Program implements a selection process to determine program finalists. The WHLD Program will extend acceptance invitations to 10-20 finalists.

Nomination and Selection Timeline

WHLDP Program Timeline

  • February 6, 2020 – Call for nominations
  • April 15, 2020 ⎼ Self-nominations per supervisor’s approval due to WHLDP
  • May 15, 2020 – Agency nominations due to WHLDP
  • June 1, 2020 – WHLDP selection process begins
  • July 17, 2020 – Placement interviews close
  • July 20-31, 2020 ⎼ Executive Leadership Committee Interviews
  • August 3, 2020 – Candidate notification of selection in the program
  • August 12, 2020 – Onboarding process of finalist begins
  • October 7, 2020 – Formal onboarding begins

For more information

  • Agencies that are interested in nominating candidates should contact whldp@gsa.gov for more information.
  • Individuals who are interested in becoming a WHLD Program Fellow should contact their agency’s Chief Human Capital Officer’s Office to learn about their agency nomination process.
