Fed2Fed Solutions

The PIC team previously provided services primarily to the performance community. We have come together with the Unified Shared Services Management Team (USSM) and the Council’s data analytics team to create a new group we call Fed2Fed Solutions to provide the same services to agency leaders, the C-Suite, and their staff, now in an even more integrated way.

What do we help our customers address?

 

  • President’s Management Agenda
  • Cross-Agency Priority Goals
  • Documented CXO Council goals or priorities
  • Governmentwide policy implementation
  • Mission support transformation projects
  • Agency Strategic Objectives & Strategic Goals
  • Agency Priority Goals
  • GAO High Risk List
  • IG Management Challenges
  • Pilot projects with potential governmentwide application

 

How We Work

We consider our customers our partners.
Together, we help agencies tackle today’s high-priority improvement and transformation challenges
by leveraging leading practices, tools, and data
while building the culture, capabilities, and conditions to continue improving tomorrow.


 

Here are a few examples of the types of Agency Challenges we hear. Each is paired with an example of a F2F Solutions service that we might recommend and provide to you. (This is not an exhaustive list and we quite frequently combine multiple solutions to address a single agency challenge.)


 

Agency Challenge

“I’ve been asked to lead a new cross-cutting initiative that brings together different parts of my Agency and don’t know where to begin.”


F2F Sample Solution

Our facilitators can help your cross-cutting team to define  the vision, strategies, and goals, and decide on performance measures to track your success.

Agency Challenge

“We want to undertake a major transformation, but past efforts have failed and I don’t trust industry.”
 

F2F Sample Solution

Our subject matter experts help you identify lessons learned from previous efforts across government and plan for success using our tested framework and best practices for contracts/managing industry.

Agency Challenge

“I want our organization to be more data-driven, but we lack the analytical skills to do data analysis.”
 

F2F Sample Solution

Our data analytics team conducts preliminary analysis on government-wide data sets  to identify areas of opportunity.

Agency Challenge

“I would like to use a service provider  for some of my financial management needs, but I’m unsure of the options available to me.”
 

F2F Sample Solution

Our service migration team educates you on the various providers

and helps you determine the best strategy to meet your needs.


 

Consultations

When you contact us, we will help you discover or refine the root cause of the issue (questions & assessments), what your best path forward might be (options & strategies), specific advice (tools & actions), and referrals to others who might be able to help you. Contact Fed2FedSolutions@gsa.gov to request a consultation.

 

Then what?

“We can do it ourselves”

In most consultation areas, we can provide you with what you need to handle the issue on your own,

such ad hoc coaching, a short DIY guide, a more robust toolkit, or another information resource.

 

“We can learn to do it ourselves”

In some cases, we may provide some train-the-trainer type training and/or periodic advice or support

so that you can learn to effectively & successfully use our

guides, toolkits, workshops, training materials, and programs

to customize & implement your own solutions.

 

“We could use some help in making progress”

And in other cases, it may be appropriate for us to engage in a more involved partnership over a period of time,

using data & analysis, facilitated engagement, hands-on workshops, working group participation,

to help you convene across perceived boundaries, work through obstacles, and generate momentum.


 

Why should we seek consultations and assistance from Fed2Fed Solutions?

  • We are Federal employees, just like you, so we understand your obstacles (and your opportunities!)
  • We bring objectivity and a neutral view of issues you may be too close or too busy to see clearly
  • We have direct access to many of the people driving large-scale change
  • We work across silos and access to a diverse network of subject matter experts and leaders
  • We invest in the capabilities of  your teams while we help you address your priorities
  • We are supported by Executive Councils so our assistance comes at no additional cost
