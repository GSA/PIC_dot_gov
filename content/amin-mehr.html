Amin Mehr

Digital Services Director
amin.mehr@gsa.gov
  • 
  • 

Amin is a technology specialist at the PIC, where he is engaged on expanding technology as a tool to better serve agencies.

In the past he has worked on a variety of high visibility cross agency technology initiatives (at the SBA, Department of Commerce and GSA) that significantly improved the public's access to government resources and services. He is also an Agile/Scrum champion and strives to lead intro to agile workshops across the government.

Amin is skilled in technology strategy, digital transformation, and open data projects. He has worked on both in the public sector and the private sector thereby giving him exposure to various channels of technology implementation.
