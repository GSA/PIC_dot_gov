Projects

Speaker Series

The PIC holds occasional Speaker Series to provide Federal performance professionals an opportunity to learn from experts in the private sector, state/local sector, academia, or agency peers and gain insight about successful approaches that are being implemented elsewhere. Sessions are held in both a live and webcast format to increase access by geographically disperse participants. Speaker Series sessions are designed to create an opportunity for agencies to come and learn as well as engage in active discussion.
