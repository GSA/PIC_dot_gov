PROJECT MGMT BONUS TOOL #1

Adopting a Growth Mindset

POSTED JUN 24, 2016

 

Happy Friday Managers!

We hope you have been finding the bi-weekly trait talks and resources enriching and interesting.

With the final four traits of the Project MGMT series, we wanted to provide some supplemental tools regarding the adoption of a “Growth Mindset” for management. Research suggests that a “Growth Mindset” can support your own development as a manager, as well as your approach to working with your team members.

Below is the first tool to help get you started with a “Growth Mindset.” The worksheet includes some brief background material and an activity that will take less than 5 minutes to complete.

PDF Worksheet: Growth Mindset_1.pdf
