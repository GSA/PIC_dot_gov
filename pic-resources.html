PIC Resources

What is Performance Management? How can it benefit you and your organization? Find the answers to these questions and more in our resources below. You can access at-a-glance information about key performance management topics in our quick guides or dive deeper into important topics in our playbooks.

Playbooks & Solutions

Submitted by kapil.bulchandani on Tue, 08/22/2017 - 19:53

Sports teams use playbooks as a set of strategies to inform how to respond to the situation on the field. Similarly, the playbooks below provide you with strategies and resources for delivering mission success.

Goal Playbook

This set of plays helps you and your team think through how to set a goal; develop a plan for the way forward; and achieve the goal.

P3 Playbook

Performance Management is a tool to help you achieve your goals, deliver the right solutions, and build the capabilities you need to evolve. The Performance Principles & Practices (P3) Playbook goes beyond legal requirements to capture the capabilities and spirit of performance management and improvement.

Fed2Fed Solutions

The PIC team previously provided services primarily to the performance community. We have come together with the Unified Shared Services Management Team (USSM) and the Council’s data analytics team to create a new group we call Fed2Fed Solutions to provide the same services to agency leaders, the C-Suite, and their staff, now in an even more integrated.
