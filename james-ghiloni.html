Mr. Jim Ghiloni is the Professional Services Program Management Executive within the Federal Acquisition Service (FAS) at the General Services Administration (GSA). In this role, he is responsible for program oversight of GSA’s Professional Services programs, including the Professional Services Multiple Award Schedules and the OASIS program. Formerly, Mr. Ghiloni served as the Director of the OASIS Program Management Office. OASIS, which stands for One Acquisition Solution for Integrated Services, is GSA’s acquisition solution that ensures that customer agencies can procure the complex, integrated professional services they need. OASIS is a comprehensive, flexible, easy to use and efficient way to obtain the best value for complex, multi-disciplinary professional service solutions with a support Information Technology component.
