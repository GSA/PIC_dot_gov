Welcome to PIC.GOV

Welcome to PIC.gov! The Performance Improvement Council (PIC) is excited to launch this site to share with you news from the federal performance management and improvement community. As a government-wide council, the PIC’s mission is to advance and expand the practice of performance management and improvement. We do this by creating opportunities where government employees working to achieve progress learn from breakthroughs achieved elsewhere and collaborate to solve complex challenges. The Council meets regularly and convenes a number of inter-agency working groups to foster dialogue and best practice sharing between federal agencies. Read more...
